Hot Shots

A welcoming school-age space where children connect with friends, explore their interests and enjoy time outside the school day.

Welcome to Hot Shots

A space to belong, create and recharge

The Hot Shots program has 30 spaces for school-aged children. Our team of Registered Early Childhood Educators and Early Childhood Assistants provides a welcoming environment where children can relax, connect with friends, and enjoy meaningful experiences before and after their school day. With a ratio of 1 educator for 15 children, educators encourage independence, leaderships, teamwork, and respect while supporting each child's interests and strengths. Guided by Ontario's How Does Learning Happen? framework and emergent curriculum, children help shape their own experiences through creative arts, STEM activities, sports, cooking, outdoor adventures, community projects, and collaborative games.

In addition to our Before and After School Program, Hot Shots also offers full-day programming on P.A. Days, March Break, Winter Break, and throughout the summer, providing exciting opportunities for children to learn, explore, and build lasting friendships in a safe and engaging environment.

Children may experience:

  • Sports and active games
  • Art, building and creative projects
  • Board games and group challenges
  • Child-led clubs and special interests
  • Quiet reading and relaxation
  • Outdoor play and community experiences

Our Approach

Children help shape the program

Children’s interests, ideas and feedback are used to plan activities, projects and special experiences that are meaningful to the group.
