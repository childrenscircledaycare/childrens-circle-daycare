Accessibility Information

Children's Circle Daycare is committed to providing an inclusive, welcoming, and accessible environment for children, families, employees, and visitors.

Statement of Organizational Commitment

Children's Circle Daycare is committed to ensuring equal access and participation for people with disabilities. We believe in treating all individuals with dignity and independence while removing barriers to accessibility whenever possible.

We are committed to meeting our obligations under the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA) and the Ontario Human Rights Code by providing accessible services, programs, and facilities.

Our accessibility policies promote independence, integration, equal opportunity, and inclusive participation for everyone.

If you require information in an alternate format or need accommodation, please contact our office and we will be happy to assist you.
