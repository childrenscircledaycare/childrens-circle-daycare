Sesames

A welcoming preschool environment filled with meaningful play, creativity, friendship and discovery.

Welcome to Sesames

Discovering together

The Sesames learn through hands-on experiences inspired by their interests, questions and everyday discoveries. Children are given time and space to explore materials, share ideas and develop friendships.

Educators create an inclusive environment that supports each child’s confidence, communication, creativity and sense of belonging.

Children may experience:

  • Open-ended creative materials
  • Early literacy and storytelling
  • Sensory and science exploration
  • Music and movement
  • Cooperative and imaginative play
  • Outdoor learning and active play

Our Approach

Learning from children’s interests

Educators extend children’s ideas by introducing thoughtful questions, interesting materials and opportunities to explore concepts in different ways.
