Helpful Information

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions families have about Children’s Circle Daycare.

Where are you located and what are your hours?

Children’s Circle Daycare is located at 175 Hampton Avenue, Toronto.

We are open Monday to Friday from 7:30 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.

Are meals and snacks provided?

Healthy meals and snacks are provided as part of our daily program. Our menus are designed to offer children nutritious, balanced and child-friendly food choices.

Explore Our Nutrition Program

How does the waitlist work?

Families can download and complete our waitlist application. Once the completed application has been received, a confirmation email will be sent.

Join the Waitlist

Which programs are offered?

We provide licensed childcare programs for infants, toddlers, preschoolers, kindergarten children and school-age children.

Explore Our Programs

Do children play outside?

Outdoor play is an important part of daily life at Children’s Circle. Children participate in outdoor experiences whenever weather, air quality and environmental conditions allow.

How do families receive daily updates?

Educators and centre staff communicate with families through Storypark and email. Updates may include learning stories, photographs, meals, naps, diapering information and centre announcements.

Learn About Storypark

How do you support children’s learning?

Our educators use How Does Learning Happen?, ELECT and emergent curriculum to observe children’s interests, support their development and plan meaningful learning experiences.

Learn About Our Approach

Where can families find policies and documents?

Families can access important centre policies, program documents, the Parent Handbook and other downloadable resources through our Resources section.

View Parent Resources
