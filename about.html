About Us

Supporting Children and Families for Over 50 Years

Children’s Circle Daycare is a non-profit child care centre committed to providing nurturing, inclusive, play-based care where children can learn, grow, and thrive.

Michelle Strople, Executive Director

Leadership

Meet Our Executive Director

Michelle Strople

Executive Director

Michelle Strople is proud to lead Children’s Circle Daycare with a commitment to providing a safe, nurturing, and inclusive environment where every child feels valued and every family feels welcomed.

Together with our dedicated educators and staff, Michelle is committed to supporting high-quality early learning experiences that help children grow with confidence, curiosity, and kindness.

Karen Farrell, Program Director

Leadership

Meet Our Program Director

Karen Farrell

Program Director

Karen Farrell leads the educational programming at Children’s Circle Daycare and works closely with our educators to ensure every classroom provides engaging, high-quality learning experiences.

She mentors and supports our teaching team, helps implement our play-based philosophy and How Does Learning Happen?, and guides staff in creating nurturing, inclusive environments where every child can learn, grow, and thrive.

Dolly Vo, Administrative Assistant

Administration

Meet Our Administrative Assistant

Dolly Vo

Administrative Assistant

Dolly plays an important role in supporting the day-to-day operations of Children’s Circle Daycare. Working closely with the Executive Director and Program Director, she assists with administrative tasks, family communications, enrolment support, scheduling, documentation, and daily office operations.

As one of the first points of contact for families, Dolly is dedicated to providing friendly, responsive service and helping create a welcoming experience for everyone who enters our centre.

OUR TEAM

Meet Our Dedicated Staff

Our dedicated team is the heart of Children’s Circle Daycare. Made up of experienced Registered Early Childhood Educators, Early Childhood Assistants, and caring supply staff, we work together to create a safe, nurturing, and inclusive environment where every child can thrive.

We are proud of our exceptionally low staff turnover, with team members who have been part of our Children’s Circle family for 5 to 45 years. Our diverse team brings a wealth of experience, cultures, and perspectives to the children and families we serve.

Curriculum

Our Approach

We believe children learn through relationships, exploration, and meaningful play. Our programs are built around Ontario’s How Does Learning Happen? framework and the ELECT emergent curriculum, allowing educators to follow children’s interests, questions, strengths, and ideas. We create welcoming environments where children build confidence, practice kindness, and develop a lifelong love of learning.
