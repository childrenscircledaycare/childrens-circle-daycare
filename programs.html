Our Educational Philosophy

Ontario's Early Learning Frameworks

Every classroom at Children's Circle Daycare is thoughtfully planned using Ontario's How Does Learning Happen? framework, the ELECT (Early Learning for Every Child Today) framework, and an emergent curriculum approach. Together, these guide the experiences, environments and relationships that support each child's learning and development.
