The Four Foundations

Programs Built Around Children: How Does Learning Happen?

Our programs are guided by Ontario’s How Does Learning Happen? framework and its four foundations for learning and development.

🤝

Belonging

Children feel connected to others and develop meaningful relationships within their family, community and environment.

💛

Well-Being

Children develop a sense of self, health and confidence through nurturing relationships, active play and responsive care.

🔎

Engagement

Children become involved and focused as they explore their interests, solve problems and investigate the world around them.

🎨

Expression

Children communicate their thoughts, feelings and ideas through language, movement, art, music and many other forms of expression.
