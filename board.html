Board of Directors

Some Background

We are a non-profit organization licensed by the City of Toronto and governed by a volunteer Board of Directors comprised of parents, community members and church members.

An Annual General Meeting is held each September to elect new members to the Board.

There are several committees that help oversee the well-being and continued operation of the daycare:

  • Finance Committee
  • Personnel Committee
  • Communications Committee
  • Policy Committee
📅

Annual General Meeting

Our Annual General Meetings are held in September. Parental involvement on the Board and its committees helps ensure the smooth operation of the daycare.

🤝

Volunteer

Children’s Circle Daycare is a non-profit charitable organization run by a Board of Directors composed of parents and representatives from St. Barnabas Anglican Church.
