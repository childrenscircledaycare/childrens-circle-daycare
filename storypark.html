Family Communication

Staying Connected with StoryPark

We use Storypark to keep families connected to their child’s day at Children’s Circle Daycare. Through regular updates, families can see important care information, classroom experiences, learning moments, photos and centre announcements.

Daily Information

A Window into Your Child’s Day

Educators use Storypark to share important daily care information. The updates provided will depend on your child’s age group and individual care needs.

🥣

Meals and Snacks

Families can receive information about lunch and snack servings, including how much their child ate.

😴

Rest and Nap Times

For children who nap, educators can record when the child rested and the length of their nap.

🧷

Diapering and Toileting

Infant and toddler families receive updates about diaper changes and other important care routines throughout the day.

🎨

Learning and Play

Educators share classroom experiences, activities, discoveries and meaningful moments from the children’s day.

Working Together

Connecting Families and Educators

Storypark supports ongoing communication between families and the daycare. Parents and guardians can view updates, celebrate their child’s learning and stay informed about classroom and centre news.

Storypark complements the conversations families have with educators at arrival and departure. Families are always encouraged to speak directly with their child’s educators when they have questions, concerns or important information to share.

💬

Communication Matters

Open and respectful communication helps us build strong partnerships with families and provide consistent, responsive care for every child.

🔒

Privacy and Access

Your Family’s Information

Storypark accounts are accessed through individual family logins. Families should keep their login information secure and contact the centre if they require assistance accessing their account.
