Welcome to the Room Above

A place to relax and be yourself

The Room Above offers school-age children a welcoming environment where they can spend time with friends, follow their interests and choose how they would like to use their time.

Educators encourage independence, responsibility and participation while remaining available to offer guidance, support and engaging opportunities.

Children may experience:

  • Creative and collaborative projects
  • Games, puzzles and group challenges
  • Sports and outdoor play
  • Reading and quiet relaxation
  • Child-led clubs and activities
  • Opportunities for leadership and responsibility

Our Approach

Choice, independence and belonging

Children are encouraged to contribute ideas, make responsible choices and help build a respectful and inclusive community.
