Welcome to Hunny Bears

Learning through curiosity and play

The Hunny Bears enjoy an engaging play-based environment designed to support creativity, communication, problem-solving and social development.

The Hunny Bear Room has 16 spaces for children aged 2 1/2 to 4 years. Our team of Registered Early Childhood Educators and Early Childhood Educators and Early Childhood Assistants create a warm, engaging environment where children are encouraged to explore, imagine, and grow through play. With a ratio of 1 educator for every 8 children, educators build meaningful relationships while supporting each child's individual interests, strengths, and stage of development.

Through hands-on experiences, creative art, sensory exploration, early literacy, music, outdoor play, and collaborative projects, children develop confidence, independence, and strong social skills. Guided by Ontario's How Does Learning Happen? framework and emergent curriculum, our educators inspire curiosity and foster a lifelong love of learning while helping children build lasting friendships and prepare for the next stage of their early learning journey.

Children may experience:

  • Art, construction and creative expression
  • Early science and nature investigations
  • Dramatic and imaginative play
  • Books, storytelling and early literacy
  • Cooperative games and group experiences
  • Daily outdoor play

Our Approach

Ideas worth exploring

Children are encouraged to ask questions, test their ideas, work together and revisit experiences over time. Their thinking and learning are celebrated throughout the classroom.
