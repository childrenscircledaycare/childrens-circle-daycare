About us

A community-based centre with deep Toronto roots.

Children’s Circle Daycare is a non-profit, community-based childcare centre serving Toronto families for over 50 years. Our experienced RECE educators create a safe, inclusive environment where children learn through play, relationships, outdoor exploration, and everyday discovery.

Healthy Meals

Nutritious Meals & Snacks Every Day

Healthy eating is an important part of every child's day. Our onsite kitchen prepares nutritious meals and snacks daily using a four-week rotating menu designed to provide children with a variety of balanced, child-friendly foods.

We proudly accommodate food allergies and dietary needs, and our menus are reviewed by a Registered Dietitian.
